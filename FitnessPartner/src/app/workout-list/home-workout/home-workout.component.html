Home Workout Program

5 days per week

Program Overview

This home workout program uses bodyweight exercises focused on strength and conditioning:

  • Upper: Push and press movements for upper body
  • Lower: Squats and lunge variations
  • Full-Body: Compound movements engaging multiple muscle groups

Note: This is a sample schedule. You can adjust the days based on your availability, but try to maintain the order and include rest days between similar workouts.

For optimal results, maintain at least 48 hours of rest between training the same muscle groups.

Target Muscles
Upper Body Day
  • Chest (pectoralis major & minor)
  • Back (latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, trapezius)
  • Shoulders (deltoids - anterior, lateral, posterior)
  • Biceps (biceps brachii, brachialis)
  • Triceps (triceps brachii)
  • Forearms (brachioradialis, flexors, extensors)
Lower Body Day
  • Quadriceps (rectus femoris, vastus lateralis/medialis/intermedius)
  • Hamstrings (biceps femoris, semitendinosus, semimembranosus)
  • Glutes (gluteus maximus, medius, minimus)
  • Calves (gastrocnemius, soleus)
  • Core (rectus abdominis, obliques, transverse abdominis)
  • Lower back (erector spinae)
