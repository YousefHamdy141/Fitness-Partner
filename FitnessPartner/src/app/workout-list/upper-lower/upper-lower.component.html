Upper/Lower Split Program

4 days per week
Program Overview

The Upper/Lower split is an efficient training method that divides your workouts into upper body and lower body sessions. This approach allows for:

  • Training each muscle group 2x per week for optimal growth
  • Sufficient recovery between sessions
  • Flexibility in scheduling (can be done in 4 days)
  • Focus on compound movements for maximum efficiency

This program is ideal for intermediate lifters who have mastered proper form on basic exercises and are looking for a structured approach to build strength and muscle.

Target Muscles
Upper Body Days
  • Chest (pectoralis major & minor)
  • Back (latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, trapezius)
  • Shoulders (deltoids - anterior, lateral, posterior)
  • Biceps (biceps brachii, brachialis)
  • Triceps (triceps brachii)
  • Forearms (brachioradialis, flexors, extensors)
Lower Body Days
  • Quadriceps (rectus femoris, vastus lateralis/medialis/intermedius)
  • Hamstrings (biceps femoris, semitendinosus, semimembranosus)
  • Glutes (gluteus maximus, medius, minimus)
  • Calves (gastrocnemius, soleus)
  • Core (rectus abdominis, obliques, transverse abdominis)
  • Lower back (erector spinae)
Upper Body Day
  • Bench Press
  • Barbell Row
  • Overhead Press
  • Pull-Ups/Lat Pulldowns
  • + Isolation exercises for arms & shoulders
8 exercises total 45-60 minutes
Lower Body Day
  • Squats or Deadlifts
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Leg Press
  • Bulgarian Split Squats
  • + Isolation exercises for calves & core
7 exercises total 45-60 minutes
